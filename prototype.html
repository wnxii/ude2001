Prototyping Techniques

Our specialized approach combines three innovative techniques designed specifically for conservation technology and community engagement

👁️

Progressive Disclosure

Complex functionality revealed gradually based on user confidence and expertise level, preventing overwhelming novice users while maintaining advanced capabilities for experts.

🌍

Local-First Design

Every interface element, content piece, and interaction pattern optimized for Glasgow's specific cultural context, species ecosystem, and community characteristics.

📊

Impact Transparency

Clear, immediate feedback connecting individual actions to measurable conservation outcomes and community goals.

Implementation Strategy

How we applied these techniques throughout our design process for maximum impact
