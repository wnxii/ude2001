Evolutionary Prototyping

Continuous refinement where the prototype evolves into the final product rather than being discarded.

Best for: Long-term development, resource efficiency

Parallel Prototyping

Creating and testing multiple different concepts simultaneously to increase design diversity.

Best for: Exploring alternatives, reducing design fixation
