Meeting #1 - Project Kickoff

22nd July 2025
Attendees

Chrissy
Friends of Glasgow Botanic Gardens

Members of Team 8

Meeting Objectives
  • ‚Ä¢ Define project objectives
  • ‚Ä¢ Clarify doubts with the project specifications
Key Discussion Points
  • ‚Ä¢ Current process of species identification
  • ‚Ä¢ Potential integration with iNaturalist for automated verification
  • ‚Ä¢ Streamlining data collection into a centralized database
Questions Asked

Q (Team): Can you walk us through step by step, through the process of recording?

A (Chrissy): An expert is tasked to manage the records of observations of species in Glasgow. This includes manually identifying the species, and recording the observations in a spreadsheet according to its location within Glasgow.

Q (Team): Who is in charge of managing the records?

A (Chrissy): There is only one expert in Glasgow who is responsible for managing the entire species identification process.

Q (Team): What is the current process of species identification requests by external parties?

A (Chrissy): An email is sent to the expert with the details of the request, including the image of the species, and the location of the observation. The expert then assesses then identifies the species based on the image and records it on to the spreadsheet.

Q (Team): What is the relevance of iNaturalist with regards to our project?

A (Chrissy): It would be good to explore how iNaturalist data can be integrated into the current process such that there is one centralised database for the species. In addition to that, requests that come in through current process could be automatically routed to iNaturalist.

Q (Team): After the iNaturalist verification process, is there a need for an extra layer of authentication?

A (Chrissy): It would not be required. However, it would be necessary to have an explanation on how iNaturalist verification works in order to be transparent.

Key Insights
  • ‚Ä¢ A single expert is responsible for the entire species identification process
  • ‚Ä¢ The current species identification process is time-consuming
  • ‚Ä¢ Integrating iNaturalist could streamline the process
Action Items
  • ‚Ä¢ Start brainstorming ideas
  • ‚Ä¢ Explore iNaturalist integration possibilities
